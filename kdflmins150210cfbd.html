1. Secretary's Report.
1.1. With reference to Item 2.1. on minutes of 13 January, Surrey County will charge member of Ewell Saxons FC. The club have now amassed 30 Disciplinary Points and, as a consequence, will appear before this committee.
1.2. KDFL Cup Final dates have been provisionaly booked for 3rd, 9th, 16th and 23rd April. Maurice Webb expressed concern over the limited time between semi-finals and finals.
1.3. Janet Ford had written to Maori Park FC reminding them that they still owed the league £50.00 in fines. The club responded saying they had no money. This matter will be forwarded to Surrey County FA.
1.4. e-mail received from David Christmas in respect of his absence from recent meetings. Following a discussion, it was agreed that Janet Ford and Malcolm Hall would format and send a reply.

2. Fixtures.
2.1. Unplayed Division 5 fixture (AFC Kingston Res. v North Leatherhead) from 7 February. AFC Kingston Res. were unable to field a team and, as per league rule 11(f), would be fined £15.00. Match Points would be awarded to North Leatherhead, who may claim reasonable expenses. This was proposed by Maurice Webb, seconded by Andy Brown and carried unanimously.
2.2. Barnslake FC in semi-final of Surrey Junior Cup and LM Utd. Res. in final of Surrey Lower Junior Cup.
2.3. Abandoned match between Petersham Pumas FC and Barnslake FC. As per league rule 10 (f) v), the result at the time of the abandonment would stand. This was proposed by Andy Brown, seconded by David Short and carried 8-0 with 1 abstention. Petersham Pumas FC would be asked to send a report to this league.

3. Treasurer.
3.1. The following sums were noted:
Current Account - £1,952.97
Deposit Account - £10,171.92
Guarantee Fund - £3,083.59
125th Anniversary Fund - £750.77
3.2. 9 clubs have a total of £315.00 in fines outstanding.
3.3. KC Chessington FC and Epsom Casuals FC have fines outstanding from November.

4. Registrations.
4.1. Some clubs naming 4 substututes on KDFL cup match cards.
4.2. Maurice Gilley will be on holiday for 2 weeks. During his absence clubs should still contact his home where his brother will handle any player registrations.

5. Clubs appearing before committee.
5.1. As per Item 1.1. (above) Ewell Saxons FC discussed disciplinary concerns.
