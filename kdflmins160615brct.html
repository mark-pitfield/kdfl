1. Chairmans's Report.
1.1. Mike Hallett asked for thoughts on Presentation Night. There was a discussion and Brian Smith estimated that £400.00 had been raised for league funds.
1.2. Mike Hallett reported that Darkside FC and Corinthians 12 FC did not attend (above) although both clubs had trophies to collect.

1.4. Lew Block and Jim Lee would be tellers at the AGM.
1.5. Some trophies are in need of repair. There was a discussion on trophies.
